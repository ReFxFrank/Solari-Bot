# Solari Premium

Premium is a **per-server** subscription that unlocks the heavy modules and higher limits.

## What's included

- **Economy** — currency, income roles, the shop, and the full [Casino](/docs/casino)
- **Music** — queue-based playback
- **Social Alerts** — Twitch, YouTube, Reddit, Bluesky & RSS
- **Temp Voice** — join-to-create channels
- **Bot Personalizer** — run Solari as your own bot
- Higher limits on custom commands and other modules

## Upgrading

Dashboard → your server → **Premium** → *Upgrade to Premium*. Checkout is handled by Stripe; the server unlocks the moment payment confirms.

## Billing privacy

Billing details — renewal date, invoices, the Stripe portal — are visible **only to the member who purchased**, not to every server admin. Other admins simply see that Premium is active.

## Managing or canceling

The purchaser opens **Manage billing** on the Premium page (Stripe's portal) to update the payment method or cancel. Canceling keeps Premium active until the end of the paid period.
